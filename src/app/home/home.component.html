Located in the heart of the opulent district of Anfa, 5 minutes from the business center, the corniche and the royal golf course, THE NESSRIN HOTEL wants to be the unmissable meeting place of the city of CASABLANCA. In an art-deco style, LE NESSRIN HOTEL is designed as an elegant and cozy private residence with its 68 rooms and suites, its magnificent Jasmin Terrace overlooking the hotel's superb garden and main swimming pool, its Bar and its refined restaurant “LA BRASSERIE DU BOULEVARD” which will undoubtedly mark your stay.
